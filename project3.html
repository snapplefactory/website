Poster Design

This project reimagines a modern object—the Baskin-Robbins ice cream tub—through the lens of the Rococo art movement. The aim was to blend the ornate elegance of Rococo with contemporary design.

The process began with designing packaging for the ice cream tub, incorporating key elements of Rococo: intricate gold detailing, ornate frames, and a pastel color palette. A mock-up of the packaging was then created to visualize how the design translated onto the product.

The final poster showcases the fusion of these two worlds, highlighting the luxurious and whimsical aesthetic of Rococo. Pastel hues, opulent gold frames, and flowing decorative patterns create a striking visual that elevates the everyday ice cream tub into a work of art, paying homage to the extravagance and charm of the Rococo era.
